Who is Shapushki?

Shapushki is the the childhood nickname of Sean Gurson, founder and CEO of Rat Race, LLC. Rat Race provides low cost web development services for businesses.

Once Shapushki learned how to develop websites, he began offering his services to local small businesses in Walnut Creek, and the rest is history.
